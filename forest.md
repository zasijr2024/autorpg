# Forest Biome

---

## Biome Identity

- Dense terrain
- High ambush frequency
- Organic materials
- Poison & control effects common

---

## Biome Affixes

| Affix | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Dense Growth | Ambush chance ↑ |
| Overgrown | Plant enemies ↑ |
| Toxic Spores | Poison chance |
| Entangling Roots | Rooted chance |
| Predatory Growth | Enemy speed ↑ |
